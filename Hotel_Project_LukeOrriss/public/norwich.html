Come and Explore Norwich!

Come and visit the UK's most preserved Medieval city! The Bishy Barnabee is in the perfect location for visiting all the sites Norwich has to offer.



Norwich Castle

Norwich castle has been standing for over 900 years. Take a tour, join a workshop or see an exhibition! Located near the city center at 24 Castle Meadow, Norwich NR1 3JU.



Dragon Hall

Take a tour of the Grade-1 listed Dragon Hall. Located near the river at 115-123 King St, Norwich NR1 1QE.



Norwich Cathedral

Visit one of the UK's talest Cathedrals located near the city centre.
