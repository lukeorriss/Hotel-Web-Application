Rooms

We have 32 beautiful rooms all with ensuite bathrooms. We provide both twin and double rooms with a choice of standard or superior facilities.



1 / 4

Standard Rooms

Twin rooms £62 per night

Double rooms £65 per night

Our standard rooms are available in twin or double configuartions. They are generously spacious and include an ensuite bathroom. Comfortable beds, triple glazed windows, and a contempory deisgn will ensure your stay is restful and enjoyable.

All our standard rooms come equipped with the following:

    32" TV
    Air Conditioning
    Hairdryer
    Kettle with complementory tea and coffee
    Fridge
    Wardrobe
    Seating Area

Wifi access and breakfast can be purchased seperately
